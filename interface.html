Menu Update System

Now it's easy to have great menuboards! Choose the day and meal you are wanting to update, type in the new menu item for the fields you wish to update, click update menu, and you're good to go! Fields left blank will stay the same. You can use the menu preview to ensure that the menu was updated properly. (*Note: Menu cannot handle " ' " characters. The menu will NOT update if that character is used.

*Please direct any bug-fixes to tklopfenstein@augie.edu
